How to Play - Guess the Cricketer

Welcome to Guess the Cricketer!

Test your cricket knowledge by identifying the cricketer based on Yes/No clues.

You will have 15 chances at Level 1, and the number of chances will decrease as you progress.

Unlock each level by guessing the correct player.

Ask strategic questions like:

  • Is the player from India?
  • Is the player a Batsman?
  • Is the player Left-handed?

If your guess is correct, you will unlock the next level!

Use your clues wisely and become the Ultimate Cricket Guru!
